Project Overview

The Smart Eco-Watcher is an innovative environmental monitoring system. It uses sensors to monitor plant health, soil moisture, and sunlight levels. The system provides real-time data and sustainability tips to help you care for your plants in an eco-friendly way.

Eco Tips for Sustainable Plant Care

  • Water plants only when necessary to save water.
  • Use natural light instead of artificial light when possible.
  • Compost plant waste instead of throwing it away.
